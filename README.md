# FitnessProgressionAndRecoverySimulation
Overview

Project Title:
Fitness Progression and Recovery in Weight Lifting Simulation

Domain:
Health and Fitness

Problem Statement:
Many individuals struggle to estimate how long it will take to achieve their
fitness goals due to variations in recovery, workout consistency, training volume,
and fatigue. This simulation aims to model fitness progression and predict how
different training decisions influence the timeline and probability of reaching
specific fitness goals.

Scope:
The simulation will model strength progression, fatigue accumulation, recovery
rates, workout consistency, and goal achievement predictions. The simulation
will not attempt to model detailed human physiology, nutrition planning, med-
ical conditions, or professional athletic performance. The focus is on general
fitness progression for recreational users.
